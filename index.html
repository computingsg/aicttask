Buzzy's Fruit School

Difficulty: Easy/Medium · Multiple choice · ~3 minutes

Buzzy is a little robot bee who works in a fruit garden. Buzzy has never seen fruit before, so the gardener teaches Buzzy which fruits are SWEET and which fruits are SOUR.

The gardener picks up fruits one at a time and tells Buzzy what each one tastes like:

FruitColourSizeTextureGardener says
🍓 StrawberryRedSmallRoughSWEET
🍋 LemonYellowSmallSmoothSOUR
🍒 CherryRedSmallSmoothSWEET
🟡 GrapefruitYellowBigSmoothSOUR

Buzzy looks at the table very carefully and notices something about the colours!

Now, a new fruit arrives: a Red Apple. It is red, big, and smooth.

Question: What will Buzzy say about the red apple?
